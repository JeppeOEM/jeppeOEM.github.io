Prime Spiraling Sticks

Description

Inspired by a video of one of my favorite creative coding teachers, Dan Schiffman. This project illustrates my workflow when dealing with creative coding.
I get inspired by a way of distrubiting values or a pattern i like, then i try to create animations with loops that a drawn in realtime, so i can se the proccess and identify places which i like.
I often work with randomness and how it affects patterns, like in this example alot of the proccess is just tweaking diffrent parameters, colors and playing with the code.
