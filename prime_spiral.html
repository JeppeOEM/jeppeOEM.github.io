Spiraling Grids

Description

Inspired by a video by one one of my favorite creative coding teachers, Dan Schiffman, on the topic of drawing a spiral that hightlights prime numbers. Often when dealing with creative coding i get inspired by a way of distributing numbers or a pattern i like, and like in this example, then craete a loop that draws frame by frame , so i can se the proccess, identify places i want expand.
In this example alot of the proccess is just tweaking diffrent parameters, colors and basicly just playing with the code.
