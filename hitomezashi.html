Description:

A Procedural drawing of a stiching pattern. After watching this numberphile video about i decided to create this pattern using p5.js.
The concept is to create two arrays of randomly generated populated arrays, one for each axis, and then draw a sequence of lines and spaces either with an offset or not depending on wether the value from the array is even or uneven.
